Electromagnets
Energy and Environmental Engineering

Isaiah Whitehorse
Grade:
7

Teacher:
Ms. DeLaGuerra

Marybelle McCorkle Academy of Excellence Science Fair 3rd Place
An electromagnet using both a 1.5 volt battery and two 1.5 volt batteries used together as 3 Volts were used to test the created electromagnets strength by picking up paper clips. The two batteries used together picked up more weight (paperclips).
Keywords
Electromagnet
1.5 Volts, 3 Volts
