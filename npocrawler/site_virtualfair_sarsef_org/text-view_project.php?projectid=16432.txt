7-CH018
Lava Lamp
Chemical Science

Luzmar Salcido
Grade:
7

Teacher:
Ms. Alejandra Alvarez

Marybelle McCorkle Academy of Excellence 2nd Place in Science Fair
Vegetable oil and Olive oil are mixed with water dyed blue. Alka seltzer is added to both oils with the water. The olive oil, water, and Alka seltzer fizzed, then the blue water sank to the bottom. The other oil had the blue color floating in it.
Key Words:
 Alka Seltzer Lava lamp olive oil
