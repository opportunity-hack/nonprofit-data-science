What Increases Heart Rate More: Jogging on an Elliptical or Jogging on a Road for Half a Mile?
Health and Wellness

Micaela Rowen
Grade:
5

Teacher:
Miss Sarah Motter

Different exercises are tested to determine which increases heart rate the most. Subjects of different gender and age completed both exercises and heart rates were measured before and after to determine change.
