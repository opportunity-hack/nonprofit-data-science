Best Nail Polish Remover
Chemical Science

Mariana Vega
Grade:
8

Teacher:
MariAnn Slater

What is your project about?
       The purpose of this experiment was to find out which nail polish remover removes nail polish the best.

Why did  you choose this project?
       I chose this project because I really enjoy painting nails, and this project helped me figure out which nail polish remover removes nail polish the best.

What was your result/overall conclusion?
In conclusion, The results of this experiment showed that OPI Nail Polish and Nail Lacquer Remover removes nail polish the best. 

Why is your project important?
My project is important because I was able to figure out which nail polish remover removes nail polsih the best.
