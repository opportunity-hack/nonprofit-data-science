3rd Place Grand Award

8-AP012
Hydroponics or Peat Moss: Which One Increases Plant Growth the Most?
Animal and Plant Science

Abril Sanchez
Grade:
8

Teacher:
MariAnn Slater

What is your project about? 
In this experiment I will grow plants using hydroponics and peat moss to find out which method best increases plant growth. 
Why did you choose this project?
I chose this experiment because I enjoy working with plants and seeing them grow.
What was your result/ overall conclusion?
My results showed that hydroponics yielded the most plant growth compared to peat moss.
Why is your project important?
My project is important because it helps farmers and other people in the agricultural field know which method of growing plants is the most efficient.
