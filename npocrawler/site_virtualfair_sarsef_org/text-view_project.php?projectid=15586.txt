What Kind of Household Materials Can Be Used to Stop/ Slow Down Apples From Browning?
Chemical Science

Rebecca Rodriguez, Alexander Sandoval
Grade:
6

Teacher:
Glenn Ocana

1. Our project is about finding what kind of substances can be used to keep apples from browning.
2. We chose this project to find which substances can help to keep apples from browning.
3. Our results were that sugar and water is the most effective.
This project has been cleared by SRC.
