3-HW001
Hand washing vs. Hand sanitizer
Health and Wellness

Nayel Huruy
Grade:
3

Teacher:
jori konrad

This project looks at the question Does hand washing really reduce the number bacteria  or virus on your hands better than hand sanitizer?
I chose this project because of the impact of COVID 19 on society.
my experiment showed that hand sanitizer reduced bacteria better than hand washing.
To keep us save from things like COVID 19
