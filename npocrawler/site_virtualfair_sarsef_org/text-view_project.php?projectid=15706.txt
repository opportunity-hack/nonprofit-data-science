7-AP006
Do Donkeys Taste the Same as Humans?
Animal and Plant Science

Olive Parisi
Grade:
7

Teacher:
Alexandria Vaughn

What is your project about?
My project is about if humans and donkeys taste food the same. 



Why did you choose this project?

I chose this project because learning about the anatomy of different animals and comparing them to humans interests me. 

What was your result/overall conclusion?

My result was that humans and donkeys have an overall similar taste perception. 

Why is your project important?

My project is important because it can help vets understand how donkeys' sense of taste works.
