The Tiny Hang Up
Engineering, Robotics and Computer Sciences

My dad always asks me to help my little sister hang up her clothes and her room is always a mess because she can't reach the hang up bar! So I invented a solution so I don't have to help her anymore! The Tiny Hang Up is made for short people who want to be able to hang up their own clothes!
