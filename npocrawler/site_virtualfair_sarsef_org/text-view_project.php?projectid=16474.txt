2-CH006
The Story of Our Skittles Experiment
Chemical Sciences

Aleah Gastelum, Victoria Gonzalez
Grade:
2

Teacher:
Ms. Feven Afewerki

McCorkle Science Fair 2nd Place for 1st/2nd Grade
Skittles were dissolved in apple cider vinegar, white vinegar and water to compare whether water or vinegar makes a better rainbow in the liquid on the plate as the skittles dissolve. Vinegar and water also were tested to see which one dissolved the Skittles faster. Water made the better rainbow; vinegar dissolved faster.
Key Words:
Skittles, Vinegar, Rainbow, Water
