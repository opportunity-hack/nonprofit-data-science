7-EA013
Under Pressure
Earth and Environmental Science

Christine Chiong, Abigail Deyo, Alyssa Seehaver
Grade:
7

Teacher:
Rita Marie Wilhite

McCorkle Academy of Excellence Science Fair 3rd Place
A candle and a lighter are used to heat air under a glass jar that's placed in a bowl of water. When the candle and lighter go out, the amount of water pulled into the glass jar from the change in air pressure is compared.
Key Words
Pressure Air Water Candle
