HS-HB013
Effect of Different Drinks on the pH of Saliva
Health and Biomedical Science

Sophia Aitken
Grade:
HS

Teacher:
Elyse Wexler

This project aimed to test the affect of different drinks on salivary pH. This information can be used to better dental health and understand dietary restrictions. In the end It was shown that coffee overall made the pH of individual's saliva more acidic.
This project has been cleared by SRC.
