7-ER015
Night-time Filter
Engineering, Robotics, and Computer Science

Arleigh Kuhn
Grade:
7

Teacher:
Ken Kratzer

A filter for cell phones at night.  The light from the cell phone is too bright and is harmful to your eyes. You can make a filter that will reduce light and make it easier on your yards. This is important because the light can damage your eye sight.
