HS-HB022
Covid-19 and the Common Cold
Health and Biomedical Science

Grace Daniel
Grade:
HS

Teacher:
Lisa Baker

Comparing the contagious surfaces and plasma transmission of T and B cells for Covid-19 and the Common Cold. This project due the biomedical interest as well as relevance. It is possible for transmission to occur but little data has been collected so far. The pandemic has greatly affected our society and scientific understanding is the key to overcoming Covid-19.
