7-HW005
The Effect of Different Food Types on Blood Glucose
Health and Wellness

Ella Bailey
Grade:
7

Teacher:
Alexandria Vaughn

What is your project about?
My project is about the effect of different food types on blood glucose.

Why did you choose this project?
I chose this project because I am a Type 1 Diabetic. A lot of people do not understand how different foods affect blood glucose.


What was your result/overall conclusion?
That different types of food really do affect blood glucose.


Why is your project important?
It can impact the way that people cover insulin for their food.
