5-CH002
The pH of Shampoos
Chemical Sciences

Chloe Aguilar
Grade:
5

Teacher:
Miss Sarah Motter

Does the price of shampoo affect the pH of the shampoo?  Hair has an ideal pH and shampoos are designed to match that. This project studies if the price of shampoos influence their pH.
