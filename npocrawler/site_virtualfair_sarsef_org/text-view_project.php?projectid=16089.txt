3-ER013
Drawerganizer
Engineering, Robotics and Computer Sciences

Daniela Campbell
Grade:
3

Teacher:
Ms. Easterling

Have you ever had so many things in your drawer that don't have a place? Do you feel like your drawers need some organization?
Do you ever have to search through a drawer to find batteries?  Or clothes? If you want to take care of the clutter without a huge price tag, get the Drawerganizer!
Anyone can use this product to turn a regular drawer into a nicely divided drawer to organize your things! It can be your clothes, your toys, or just a junk drawer. It has a very low price tag and anyone can install it in minutes! The best part is that is can be removed and replaced with no harm to the existing drawer!
Look no further than the Drawerganizer to get rid of the clutter!
