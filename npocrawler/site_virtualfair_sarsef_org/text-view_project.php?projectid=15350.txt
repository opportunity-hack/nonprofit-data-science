3rd Place Grand Award

HS-HB011
The Effectiveness of Copaiba (The Effects of Copaifera reticulata on Gram-positive and Gram-negative Bacteria)
Health and Biomedical Science

Gabriel Bojorquez, Ricardo Villarreal
Grade:
HS

Teacher:
Vangielyn Dela Cruz

1. Our project is about Copaiba oil against gram-positive and gram negative bacteria.
2. We chose this project because of the benefits Copaiba oil can provide for our health.
3. Our results were that the oil reduced the growth of gram positive bacteria but not the gram-negative bacteria.
4. Our project is important in terms of improving treatments in medicine.
This project has been cleared by SRC.
