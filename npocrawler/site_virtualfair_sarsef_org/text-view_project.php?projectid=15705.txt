What is your project about?
It is about the coefficient of friction. 



Why did you choose this project?
I chose this project because I was thinking about our world and how to make engines better for the world. 


What was your result/overall conclusion?
My result was that petroleum jelly had the highest coefficient of friction. 


Why is your project important?
My project is important because it helped me know what kind of things had the best coefficient of friction.
