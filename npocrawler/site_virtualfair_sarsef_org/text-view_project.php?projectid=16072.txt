7-ER016
Techno Moon - Thriving on the Moon
Engineering, Robotics, and Computer Science

Andrea Maestas, Roman Switzer Jr., Xennoveva Zepeda
Grade:
7

Teacher:
Jackie Nichols

Techno Moon is a city of lunar lights, high tech electronics, and dreams! People live in the city of Techno Moon because it takes them away from the natural disasters and life on Earth. Come to Techno Moon for a fresh start in life. Keywords: lunar, energy, oxygen, zoning, transportation, regolith, water ice, high-tech, artificial intelligence.
