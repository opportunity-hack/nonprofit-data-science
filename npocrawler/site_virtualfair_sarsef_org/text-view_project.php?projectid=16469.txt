2-CH005
Mentos Geyser Project
Chemical Sciences

Shelby Bishop, Lorenzo A. Brusoe
Grade:
2

Teacher:
Ms. Feven Afewerki

McCorkle Science Fair 1st Place in 1st/2nd 
Mentos was tested with diet coke and citrus acid (lemon lime ) sodas at room temperature and cold temperatures to see which would make the tallest geyser. The Mentos were also broken, crushed and tested to see what the effect on the height of the geyser would be.
