Slow Gravity
Engineering, Robotics, and Computer Science

Maximus Domin

Inspired by the recent Perseverance Mars rover mission, this project focuses on parachute research. Testing and comparing geometric shapes with a similar volume to determine if a parachute's canopy affects the speed of a payload's descent from deployment to landing.
