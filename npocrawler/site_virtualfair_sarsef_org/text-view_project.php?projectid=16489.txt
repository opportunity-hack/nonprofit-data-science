8-HW001
Which Toothpaste Helps Whiten Teeth With Braces Best?
Health and Wellness

Briana Munoz
Grade:
8

Teacher:
MariAnn Slater

What is your science project about?
My science project is about finding out which toothpaste whitens teeth with braces the best.
Why did you choose this project?
As someone who wears braces, this topic is important to me. Therefore, this experiment helps determine which toothpaste is most effective when brushing teeth with braces.
What was your result/overall conclusion?
In conclusion, Colgate Optic White Renewal Teeth Whitening Toothpaste whitens teeth best when having braces. 
Why is your project important?
My project is important so people with braces know which toothpaste whitens their teeth is.
