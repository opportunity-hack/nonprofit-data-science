Chemical Science

Grade:
7

What’s it about: My project is about what happens when you change the portions of ingredients
in bread.
• Why did I choose this Project: I chose this project because I wanted to know how I could bake
better bread and also cause I enjoy baking a lot.
• What was my overall conclusion: My overall conclusion was that, Yes bread ingredient portions
do change the drastically depending on what ingredient your changing

proportions you should change to make your bread come out better.
