Heads or Tails? Observing the Effects of Amoxicillin on the Regeneration Rate of Common Annelids Lumbricus terrestris  and Zophopas morio
Animal Science

David Lopez, Victor Villapuda
Grade:
HS

Teacher:
Alfred Santos

This project is about the effects of amoxicillin a common type of antibiotics on the regeneration of 2 annelid. We chose this project because we are interested with zoology. Based on the results, we found out that antibiotics has an effect on  the regeneration rate of annelids
