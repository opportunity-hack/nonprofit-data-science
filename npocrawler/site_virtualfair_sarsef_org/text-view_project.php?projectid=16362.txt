Which Brand of Batteries Last the Longest?
Physics, Astronomy, and Mathematics

Ayden Rivera
Grade:
6

Teacher:
MariAnn Slater

Problem
Which Brand of Batteries Last the Longest?

Why did I choose this experiment?
I chose this experiment because I love to play video games and I needed to know which battery was best for my controllers.

Results
My results were that Duracell lasted the longest.

Why is it important
This experiment is important because it lets me know what battery I should use for my controllers.
