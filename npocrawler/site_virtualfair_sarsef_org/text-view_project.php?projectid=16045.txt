7-EE016
City of Stars Against the Problems of a Lunar Ecosystem
Energy and Environmental Engineering

Jazmin Echegaray, Patricia Machi Pollerena, Johannah Urbina
Grade:
7

Teacher:
Jackie Nichols

Our project is about the challenge of building a city on the moon. We are part of the Future City class and the challenge was using two of Moon's natural resources to design a city that is healthy and safe for people. City of stars is the future and the future is bright. City of Stars is a new step for humanity, enjoy the comfort of your home planet earth while being safe and happy on the moon.  Agriculture, energy, biomimicry, ocean, recycling, waste management, solar power, regolith, mining, space.
This project has been cleared by SRC.
