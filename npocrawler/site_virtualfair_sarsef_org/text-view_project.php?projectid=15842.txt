Does Sunlight Time Affect the Electrical Conductivity in Persian Limes?
Energy and Environmental Engineering

Viviana Portillo
Grade:
HS

Teacher:
Elyse Wexler

1. This project is about how heat affects the electrical conductivity in Persian limes.
2. This project was chosen to find the way a new resource of electricity can be affected by the environment.
3. There is a negative correlation between electrical conductivity in Persian limes with spikes about every 10 minutes of peak electricity.
4. This project is important because with constant decreasing amounts of fossil fuels renewable resources are on the rise, fruits being a possibility in the future.
