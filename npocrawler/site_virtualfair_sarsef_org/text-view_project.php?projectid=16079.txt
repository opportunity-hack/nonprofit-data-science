New Luna City - The City of Stars
Energy and Environmental Engineering

Michael Bramlett, Julian Gomez, Sean Griffin
Grade:
8

Teacher:
Jackie Nichols

We are trying to find a solution to all the problems that come with living on the moon. We are attempting to build a sustainable city that can support people living on the moon. Keywords: helium, regolith, oxygen, waste recycling, robotics, moon.
