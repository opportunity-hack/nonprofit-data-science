HS-EL003
The Smart Toilet - An Automated Urine Screening Device for Kidney Failure, Bladder Cancer, and Prostate Enlargement
Electrical and Mechanical Engineering

Hailey Chualmorkodt
Grade:
HS

Teacher:
Patricia Garcia

The SMART TOILET is an augmented toilet with multiple specialized sensors. These sensors measure: (1) total urine volume, (2) urine rate, and (3) blood in urine.  The SMART TOILET improves early detection of Bladder Cancer, Kidney Failure, and Prostate Enlargement.
This project has been cleared by SRC.
