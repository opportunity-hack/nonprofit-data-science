HS-BE001
Tossing and Turning: Investigating the Impact of Somatic vs. Cognitive Symptoms of Anxiety on Sleep Quality in Teenagers
Behavioral and Social Science

Tessa Steven Wuelpern
Grade:
HS

Teacher:
Jeremy Jonas

-sleep psychology
-anxiety
-mental health
-survey

1. Correlation between anxiety symptom type and sleep quality
2. Personal experience with sleeplessness and anxiety symptoms, as well as interest in sleep psychology and mental health
3. Data pending
4. Identifying correlations between anxiety symptom type and sleep quality can help individuals understand their specific needs based on their majority symptom type. Also, mental health related experiments can help to break down the stigma around mental health and normalize that it impacts everyone.
This project has been cleared by SRC.
