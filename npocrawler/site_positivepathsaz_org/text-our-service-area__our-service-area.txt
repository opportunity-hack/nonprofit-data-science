Service Area

Our service area includes the cities and towns of
Scottsdale, Fountain Hills, Tempe, Mesa, Chandler, Gilbert, Queen Creek,
San Tan Valley, Apache Junction, and unincorporated areas
within these community boundaries.
For the purposes of our Scholarship and Mentoring program,
women must be residents of one of these communities
at the time of application and continuously while involved.
