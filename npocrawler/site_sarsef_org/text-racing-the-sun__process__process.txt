Before building
1. Teachers are oriented to the program and gather their teams
2. Participants learn the basics at Workshops Day
3. Participants submit release forms
Learn more






While designing and building
1. Students plan, design, and build their carts
2. Students present their progress to mentors and make changes through iterations
3. Students visit companies and universities on Career Day
Learn more






After building
1. Teams undergo safety checks and conduct trial runs on Test Day
2. Teams present their work to industry judges
3. Teams compete on Race Day
Learn more
