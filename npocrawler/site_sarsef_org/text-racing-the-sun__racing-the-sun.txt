Racing the Sun
Registration Deadline – September 17th, 2021
Racing the Sun is an exciting engineering program that challenges high school students to design, build, and race solar-powered go-karts. It is open to all Arizona high school students.
 About the process
Important dates









Register a team
Teams come in many forms including CTE course-based, engineering high school classes, after-school clubs, or community teams.
Registration closes September 17, 2021.
 Register your team






How it works


Before building
Teachers are oriented to the program and gather their teamsParticipants learn the basics at Workshops DayParticipants submit release forms


While Designing and Building
Students plan, design, and build their cartsStudents present their progress to mentors and make changes through iterationsStudents visit companies and universities on Career Day


After building
Teams undergo safety checks and conduct trial runs on Test DayTeams present their work to industry judgesTeams compete on Race Day






Challenges and skills
Students spend nine months preparing for Race Day with the support of their teachers and mentors. Along the way, they apply concepts from physics, engineering, and energy to solve real-world problems through collaboration within their teams.
